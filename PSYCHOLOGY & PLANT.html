Psychology and Plant

Pothos

This plant gives a feeling of peace thanks to its flowing, soft, heart-shaped leaves, making it the perfect addition to your home or office.

Saves from bad energy

String of Pearls

This houseplant is a soft and elegant choice. With its light and airy appearance, it adds a sense of calm to even the busiest of days.

Eases trauma

Alocasia

The beautiful alocasia plant features thick green leaves with a silvery coating, making it an elegant addition to your houseplant collection.

Improves mood

Lavender

Lavender is an herbaceous plant with yellow or pinkish flowers, known for its gentle aromatic scent. It is considered one of the best essential oils for calming nerves and improving relaxation.

Improves relaxation

Chamomile

Chamomile is used to calm the nervous system and improve sleep. Its oval-shaped flowers and green leaves make it a soothing presence in any space.

Calms the nervous system

Peppermint

Peppermint is a natural stimulant used to boost alertness and reduce mental fatigue. Its fresh minty scent invigorates the senses.

Reduces mental fatigue
